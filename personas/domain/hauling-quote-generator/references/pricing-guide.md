## Price Increase Triggers

Flag these for potential price adjustment after on-site assessment:

- More items than photos showed
- Heavier items than expected
- Discovered special disposal items
- Access more difficult than described
- Hoarder/unsanitary conditions
- Items scattered (not consolidated)
- Weather complications
- Customer not ready/delays
