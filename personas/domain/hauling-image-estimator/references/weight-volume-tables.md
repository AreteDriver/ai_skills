## Truck Capacity Reference

### Standard 16-Yard Dump Truck
- **Volume capacity:** 16 cubic yards (432 cu ft)
- **Weight limit:** 4-6 tons (varies by truck)
- **Practical fill:** 70-80% = 11-13 cubic yards
